On the roadmap

Deployment options, coming soon

Enterprise-grade deployment is in active development. Here's what's coming — built for teams that need full control over where their substrates live and who can access them.

Private Registry

Host your own substrate registry behind your firewall — with SSO, RBAC, audit logs, and a custom domain. Your proprietary blueprints never touch the public registry.

Self-hostable

Deploy the full Hylé registry stack as a single binary or Docker container — no external dependencies. Designed for air-gapped and regulated environments.

Org Namespace

Claim a verified @org/ namespace on the public registry. Publish, discover, and govern substrates across your entire engineering org — with member management and pull analytics built in.
